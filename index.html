Who we are

The Plate Restaurant | Karjat

Your go-to veg spot in Karjat, serving fresh, local goodness. Come for the food, stay for the experience!
Celebrating the Art of Vegetarian Cooking At The Plate, we believe that vegetarian cuisine is more than just a diet – it’s a lifestyle that connects us to the earth. With a focus on sustainability, we source the freshest produce to create dishes that are not only delicious but also wholesome. Blending traditional recipes with modern twists, our menu promises to deliver a delightful dining experience in the picturesque surroundings of Karjat. Our Story Founded by a team of passionate food lovers, The Plate is built on a commitment to serve nutritious, flavorful, and beautifully presented vegetarian dishes. Whether you’re a long-time vegetarian or simply looking to enjoy a plant-based meal, we welcome you to savor the flavors of nature with us.

What we do

Events

Host Your Special Events with The Plate Looking for the perfect venue to host a vegetarian celebration? Whether it’s a birthday, anniversary, or corporate retreat, The Plate offers a warm and inviting setting with menus tailored to your event. Our expert team will handle every detail, ensuring that your event is a memorable one. Book Your Event Now

Corporate Events

Host your next corporate gathering at The Plate, where professionalism meets exquisite vegetarian cuisine. With tailored menus and a serene ambiance, it’s the perfect setting for team-building sessions, client meetings, or company celebrations. .

Themed Food Festivals

Dive into the flavors of India’s diverse vegetarian cuisine with our vibrant food festivals, each themed to celebrate regional specialties and seasonal delights. From street food to festive feasts, enjoy a culinary journey like no other at The Plate. .

Customized Celebration Dinners

Make your special moments unforgettable with a fully personalized dining experience at The Plate, designed to suit any occasion. From bespoke menus to custom décor, we ensure every detail reflects yourunique style and taste..
